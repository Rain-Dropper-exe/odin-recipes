Brownie Thick Shake

Description

This brownie thick shake is the perfect dessert drink! Rich, creamy, and packed with chocolate brownie chunks, it’s a chocolate lover’s dream come true.

Ingredients

  • 2 large brownies, crumbled
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream for topping

Steps

  1. Blend the crumbled brownies, ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup until smooth.
  2. Pour into a tall glass and top with whipped cream and extra brownie crumbles.
  3. Serve chilled and enjoy!
