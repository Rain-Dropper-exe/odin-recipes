Shawarma

Description

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made of seasoned meat that's cooked on a vertical rotisserie. It's flavorful, juicy, and perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or lamb, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices and olive oil for at least 2 hours.
  2. Cook the chicken on a grill or in a pan until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  3. Serve with pita bread, garlic sauce, and veggies.
